TOWERS

SLOP DEFENSE
Buzzwords are attacking your castle. Fight back with absurd towers and a red pen.

HOW TO PLAY

  1. Click a tower in the sidebar, then click the map to place it
  2. Towers auto-attack orange slop enemies marching along the path
  3. Click-and-drag the RED PEN to slash enemies directly (12s cooldown)
  4. Click cyan GOOD WORDS for power-ups, or let them pass for gold
  5. Keep your towers clean -- they get corrupted by nearby slop!
  6. Watch out for BUZZWORD FUSION -- clustered enemies merge into monsters

TOWERS

🐕 Dog - Reliable damage, no friendly fire
🤓 Well Actually - High damage, slight friendly fire
🦆 Rubber Duck - Bounces, reveals stealth
🏗️ Trebuchet - Massive splash damage
☕ Coffee - Speeds up nearby towers
🧌 Troll - Chaotic damage, random shots

● Thought Leader - Fast swarm
● Stealth Slop - Disguised
● Slop Bomb - Explodes on death
● Boss - Big threat
