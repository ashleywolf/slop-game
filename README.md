# SLOP Defense

Tower defense, but the enemies are corporate buzzwords and your towers get corrupted.

**[Play it](https://ashleywolf.github.io/slop-game/)**

Place towers on a grid. Enemies -- Buzzwords, Corporate Phrases, Thought Leaders, Stealth Slop -- march toward your castle. Towers auto-attack, but nearby enemies corrupt them. Clustered enemies fuse into bigger monsters. You have a red pen on a 12-second cooldown for manual strikes.

6 tower types: Dog (reliable), Well Actually (high damage), Rubber Duck (bounces), Trebuchet (splash), Coffee (speed buff), Troll (chaotic). 15 waves. Good luck with the boss.

## How it works

- 20x14 grid-based tower placement
- 6 tower types, 6 enemy types, corruption mechanic
- Enemy fusion system for clustered mobs
- Combo multiplier for consecutive hits
- Power-up words (cyan) for buffs or gold
- Canvas 2D rendering, 15-wave progression
- Vanilla JS, no dependencies, single HTML file
